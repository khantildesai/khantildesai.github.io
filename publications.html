Sensing of the Self, Society, and the Environment

Based on these applications and other aspects of the OpenEyeTap, a paper in the IEEE Sensors Journal was published that outlined the abilities of such open-source smart wearable technologies. This paper outlines the levels of connectedness of these technologies and the types of interactions they facilitate and provides examples of each type of system implemented on the OpenEyeTap device. That paper is available at: http://wearcam.org/ieeesensors2020/IEEE_Sensors_Sensing_Self_Technology_Society_and_Environment/PID6605899.pdf?mc_cid=3900f52874
